Bluth's Banana Stand

Welcome to the Stand

Welcome to Bluth's Frozen Banana Stand! We have been operating for almost 50 years, which practically makes us a Newport Beach landmark. Our founder, George Bluth, started the stand to give the citizens of California the freshest frozen bananas possible. Today, we serve the freshest frozen bananas, but also tasty entrees, quenching beverages and sweet desserts. We hope you enjoy what we have to offer!
