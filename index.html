Bluth's Frozen Banana Stand

Welcome to the Stand

Welcome to Bluth's Frozen Banana Stand! We have been operating for almost 50 years, which practically makes us a Newport Beach landmark. Our founder, George Bluth, started the stand to give the citizens of California the freshest frozen bananas possible. Today, we serve the freshest frozen bananas, but also tasty entrees, quenching beverages and sweet desserts. Enjoy!

Bluth’s Frozen Banana Stand Anniversary

On this day, 53 years ago, George Bluth bravely went where no man had gone before: into the frozen banana business. Throughout the years, Mr. Bluth perfected the frozen banana recipe and even ventured into other banana flavored dishes. As he has come to find, there is always money in the banana stand.

Date / Time: May 1, 2016 from 1pm - 4pm

Treats: Complimentary frozen banana to help celebrate this wonderful day in history.

Mr. Manager’s Birthday

Our beloved Mr. Manager (otherwise known as George Michael Bluth) is turning 26! Mr. Manager got his start at the banana stand when he was just 15 years old, selling bananas on the weekends. His cousin Maeby and he were the best employees the banana stand has ever seen.

Today, we celebrate Mr. Manager and his triumphant return to the banana stand! He is about to graduate with his master’s degree from Juilliard in Percussion Performance.

Date / Time: May 17, 2016 from 4pm - 7pm

Treats: Complimentary frozen banana if you bring Mr. Manager a birthday card.
